Privacy Policy

INNLED is committed to ensuring that the collection and processing of your data via the website http://muza.innled.fr/ comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the French Data Protection Act.

1. Collection of personal data
The Innled website does not include any data collection forms. The only way to transmit personal data is through “mailto” contact links available on the site, allowing you to send us an email directly.

2. Purpose of processing
The data you send us by email (email address, first name, last name, message content) are used exclusively to:
• Process and respond to your request (information, quote, technical support).
• Manage any potential business relationship that may result.

These data are never used for automated processing, profiling, or shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Legal basis
The processing is based on INNLED’s legitimate interest in responding to inquiries and managing professional communications.

4. Data retention period
Data collected via email are kept for the time necessary to process your request. If the exchange does not lead to a contractual relationship, your data will be deleted within a maximum of 2 years after the last contact.

5. Data recipients
Information sent by email is intended solely for authorized personnel of INNLED. No data is transferred or sold to third parties.

6. Your rights
In accordance with European regulations, you have the right to access, rectify, and delete your personal data. You may also object to the processing of your data for legitimate reasons.

To exercise your rights, you can contact us:
• By email: contact@innled.fr
• By post: INNLED, 360 Avenue des Compagnons, 34170 Castelnau-le-Lez, France.

7. Cookies
Please note that the website may use technical cookies necessary for navigation or anonymous audience measurement tools. You can configure your browser to refuse these cookies.
